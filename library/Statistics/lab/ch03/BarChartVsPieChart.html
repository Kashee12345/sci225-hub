Bar Chart vs Pie Chart

Give the same table of counts to two charts and you get two different answers. A bar chart is built to compare categories; a pie chart is built to show each category against the whole. Neither is decoration — each one makes a specific question easy and another question hard. Every number below comes from a sample this page actually draws.
