Forms of Marriage

Marriage feels like the most private decision a person makes. Cross-culturally it is one of the most regulated. How many spouses are permitted, which way wealth travels at the wedding, and who is left holding it are questions a society usually answers long before any particular couple meets. Count the spouses first, then follow the wealth — and watch how the two answers turn out to be tied to each other.
