Beyond Words: Distance, Gesture, and Tone

Strip the words out of a conversation and a great deal of it survives — how close the speakers stand, what their hands do, how the pitch of a sentence rises or falls. None of it is instinct. All of it is learned, patterned, and local, which is why the same body in the same posture can be read three different ways in three different places. Move the controls and watch the meaning change.
