Explaining Stratification

Everyone can see that a society is unequal. The hard question is why — and sociology has never settled on one answer. Davis and Moore say inequality is a job society needs done. Marx says it is theft, built into who owns what. Weber says money is only one of three ladders, and people climb them at different speeds.
