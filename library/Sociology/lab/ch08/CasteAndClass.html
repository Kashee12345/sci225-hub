Caste & Class: Closed and Open Systems

Every society ranks its members. What separates them is a single question — can the ranking ever change? At one end sits the caste system, where position is handed to you at birth and sealed there for life. At the other sits meritocracy, a pure ideal no society has reached. Class systems live in between. Tap each ladder, then sort twelve societies and watch the tidy binary come apart.
