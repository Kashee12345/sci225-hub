Three Lenses on Gender

Sociology does not have one explanation of gender — it has three, and they disagree on purpose. The functionalist lens asks what an arrangement does for the system, the conflict lens asks who benefits from it, and the interactionist lens asks how it gets produced in an ordinary conversation.
