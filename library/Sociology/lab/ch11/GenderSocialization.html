Gender Socialization: Six Agents, One Child

Nobody is born knowing that trucks are for one child and dolls are for another, that one voice should carry across a room and another should not. Every bit of it is taught — by six agents working at different ages, through different mechanisms, with different punishments for getting it wrong.
