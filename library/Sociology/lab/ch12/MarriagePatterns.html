Marriage Patterns: Who May Marry Whom

Marriage feels like the most private decision a person ever makes. It is also one of the most heavily regulated: every society on record has rules about how many spouses you may have, which people are permanently off-limits, and which people you are quietly expected to prefer. Count the spouses, switch the rules on and off and watch your field of eligibles collapse, then follow the funnel that turns a free choice into a highly predictable one.
