Inside the Schoolhouse

Ask a school what it is for and you get the manifest functions — the ones printed in the mission statement. Ask what it actually does and you find latent functions nobody planned. Ask what a student learns there and you find the hidden curriculum: lessons taught by the shape of the room rather than by the syllabus.
