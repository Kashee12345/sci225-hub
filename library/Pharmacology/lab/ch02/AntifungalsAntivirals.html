Antifungals & Antivirals: Where They Strike

Bacteria are easy to poison — they're built differently than we are. Fungi are eukaryotes like us, and viruses hide inside our own cells, so these drugs have far fewer safe targets — and that narrow margin is exactly why their toxicities matter.
