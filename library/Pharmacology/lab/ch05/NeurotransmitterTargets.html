Neurotransmitters & Drug Targets

Almost every psychiatric, antiepileptic, and anesthetic drug does one small thing at one small place in a synapse — it blocks a pump, blocks an enzyme, or sits on a receptor. Learn the six transmitters and their machinery and the drug classes stop being a list to memorize. Tap a transmitter, then tap a drug class to light up its target.
