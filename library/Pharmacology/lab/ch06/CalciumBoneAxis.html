Calcium, Vitamin D & the Bone Axis

Bone is not scaffolding — it is a calcium bank that is torn down and rebuilt every day, and the body will rob it without hesitation to keep the serum calcium in range. Every drug in this section does one thing at one address on that loop: the gland that senses calcium, the gut that absorbs it, the kidney that activates vitamin D, the cell that breaks bone down, or the cell that builds it back.
