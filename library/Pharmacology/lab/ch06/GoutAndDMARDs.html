Gout & Antiarthritis Drugs: Three Different Jobs

Three groups of drugs get used on painful joints, and they are not interchangeable. One group stops an attack that is happening now. One lowers uric acid over months and does nothing for the pain in front of you. One slows the destruction of a rheumatoid joint.
