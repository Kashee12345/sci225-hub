The Axis & the Off-Switch

Every hormone in this chapter is governed by the same three-story loop: the hypothalamus signals the pituitary, the pituitary signals a target gland, and the hormone that comes out climbs back up and shuts its own bosses off. That loop cannot tell the difference between the hormone a gland made and the hormone a nurse handed over — so every dose you give is also a message to stop making it.
