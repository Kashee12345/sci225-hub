Supply vs Demand: Nitrates & the ACS Bundle

Angina is one sentence of physiology: the myocardium is asking for more oxygen than the coronary arteries are delivering. Every drug in the acute coronary syndrome bundle attacks that sentence from one side or the other — it either lowers demand or raises supply. Tap each order to give it or withhold it, and watch which side of the ledger it actually moves.
