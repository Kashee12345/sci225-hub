Male Hormones & ED Drugs

Two drug stories meet in one older male patient: testosterone for low androgen, and the PDE-5 inhibitors (sildenafil, tadalafil) for erectile dysfunction. The PDE-5 drugs raise cGMP to relax vessels — and so do nitrates. Stack the two and blood pressure can collapse.
