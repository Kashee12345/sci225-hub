The Vomiting Pathway

Nausea and vomiting are a reflex: four separate triggers feed one vomiting center in the medulla, and each trigger speaks through different receptors. That is the whole secret of antiemetics — you don't sedate the whole brain, you block the receptor of the trigger that is driving this patient.
