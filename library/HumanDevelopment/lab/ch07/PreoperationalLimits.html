Conservation & Egocentrism

In Piaget's preoperational stage (about ages 2–7), children think in symbols but not yet in logical operations. Two hallmarks reveal the limits: they fail conservation, and their thinking is egocentric. Pick a classic task, run the change, and watch the error.
