The Moral Reasoning Ladder

Lawrence Kohlberg tracked how our values mature — not what we decide, but why. He posed a dilemma: a man named Heinz can't afford the one drug that would save his dying wife, so should he steal it? Climb his six stages, grouped into three levels, and watch a teen's reasoning grow up.
