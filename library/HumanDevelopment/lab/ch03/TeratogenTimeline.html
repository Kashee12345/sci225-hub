Teratogens & the Prenatal Timeline

A teratogen is any agent — a drug, infection, chemical, or radiation — that can disrupt prenatal development. But timing matters as much as the substance: the same teratogen can be devastating one week and nearly harmless the next. Drag a teratogen onto the timeline to find its window of greatest harm.
