The Body at Its Peak

Early adulthood — roughly the twenties and thirties — is the body's physical prime: strength, speed, fertility, and reserve all sit near their lifetime high. But "peak" also means the slow decline is about to begin, and the habits set now decide how steep it is. Tap a system to see when it crests and how to protect it.

Primary vs. secondary aging. Some decline is primary — biological, gradual, and universal. But much of what ages a body is secondary — driven by lifestyle and disease, and largely under your control. Early adulthood is where those choices start to compound.
