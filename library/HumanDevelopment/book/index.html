Human Development

the lifespan, explained plainly

A journey across the human lifespan — from a single cell to late adulthood, told in plain English: how we grow, think, feel, bond, and change at every age. Real figures, comparison tables, insight boxes, and a self-check in every chapter.

by Dr. Vidya Aggrawal · Nightingale College
🌱 A note: the book runs 14 chapters — from studying development and the great theories, through prenatal life, infancy, childhood, and adolescence, into early, middle, and late adulthood, and finally death and dying. Each chapter explains the idea plainly, shows a real figure, and ends with a self-check.
