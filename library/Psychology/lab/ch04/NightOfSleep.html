A Night of Sleep

Sleep isn't one flat state — across a night the brain repeatedly sinks into deep NREM sleep and rises into dreaming REM. A chart of those stages over time is called a hypnogram. Press Next to walk through one whole night, or tap any part of the chart to jump there.
