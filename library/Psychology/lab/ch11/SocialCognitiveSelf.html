Shaped By, Shaping Back

The social-cognitive approach — led by Albert Bandura and Julian Rotter — locates personality in the space between mind, action, and world. It rejects the idea that you are simply pushed by drives or pulled by rewards; instead you are an active agent who shapes the very forces that shape you. Explore its two core ideas below.
