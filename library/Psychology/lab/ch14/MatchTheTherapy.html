Match the Therapy

Every technique carries the fingerprint of the school that invented it. Psychodynamic therapy digs for the unconscious, humanistic therapy offers acceptance, behavioral therapy re-conditions actions, and cognitive therapy reframes thoughts. Read each technique, tap the school it belongs to, and watch the consulting room change to fit.

The four lenses: Psychodynamic (Freud) uncovers the unconscious; Humanistic (Rogers, Maslow) offers acceptance so growth unfolds; Behavioral (Pavlov, Skinner, Wolpe) reconditions behavior; Cognitive (Beck, Ellis) reframes thinking. Combine the last two and you get CBT.
