The Forgetting Curve

In 1885 Hermann Ebbinghaus memorized lists of nonsense syllables and tracked how fast he lost them. Memory decays steeply at first, then levels off — but each review resets the curve and makes forgetting slower. Drag the day marker and build a review schedule to see it.
