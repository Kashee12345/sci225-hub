Which Fuel, When

A muscle cell can only spend ATP, and it holds barely two seconds’ worth. Everything else — the phosphagen system, glycolysis, oxidative phosphorylation — exists to rebuild that ATP as fast as it is spent. Which system does the rebuilding is not a choice. It is decided by how hard and how long. Move those two dials and watch the handover happen.
