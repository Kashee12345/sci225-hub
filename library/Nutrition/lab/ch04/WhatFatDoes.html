What Fat Actually Does

Fat is usually introduced as the concentrated fuel — 9 kcal per gram against 4 for carbohydrate and protein. That is true, and it is the smallest part of the story. Lipid builds every membrane you own, insulates every nerve, is the raw material for steroid hormones, and is the only vehicle that gets vitamins A, D, E and K across the gut wall.
