The Food Security Spectrum

Food security is not on-or-off — it is a continuum. The United States Department of Agriculture measures it with a household survey and sorts the answers into four levels, from high to very low food security. Slide the number of survey items a household reports and watch it move along the spectrum — and watch exactly what changes at each step.
