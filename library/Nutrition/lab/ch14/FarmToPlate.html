From Farm to Plate

Food does not appear on a plate — it travels a supply chain of five stages, and it loses mass at every one. Globally, roughly a third of all food grown for people is never eaten. Pick a food, set how much leaves the farm, and step through the chain to see where it goes — and how much water and greenhouse gas is thrown away with it.
