The Footprint of a Meal

Every food carries the land, freshwater, and greenhouse gas it took to produce it. Build a plate one serving at a time and watch its footprint recompute — and notice how one ingredient can dominate the whole meal. This is a picture of the food system, not a verdict on any food or on anyone’s eating.
