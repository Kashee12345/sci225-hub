The Added Sugar Detective

Added sugars answer to dozens of names on an ingredient list, and one product can carry four of them at once — each small enough to sit low in the list. This isn’t about good foods and bad foods. It’s a reading skill: learn the names, find them on the label, then do the arithmetic yourself.
