Count the Calories

A kilocalorie is not something a food contains — it is a measurement of the energy released when the body oxidises what the food is made of. Only four substances yield it: carbohydrate 4, protein 4, fat 9, and alcohol 7 kilocalories per gram. Type in grams below and watch the total assemble itself.
