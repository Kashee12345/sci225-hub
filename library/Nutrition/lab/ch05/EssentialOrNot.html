Essential or Not

Twenty amino acids build every protein in you, and you need all twenty. Essential does not mean more important — it means your cells have no pathway to construct the carbon skeleton, so it has to arrive in food already made. Nine are like that. Six more can normally be built, but only from a precursor you must eat, or only when demand is ordinary. Sort them yourself below.
