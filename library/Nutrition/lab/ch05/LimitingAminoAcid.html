Fill the Limiting Amino Acid

A plant food is rarely short on every indispensable amino acid — usually just one runs low, and that single limiting amino acid caps how well the whole protein can be used. Pair two plant foods whose shortfalls differ, blend the protein between them, and watch the low bar rise to the reference line.
