Substitution, Step by Step

Graphing shows you where two lines meet; substitution tells you exactly where, no ruler required. The whole method rests on one small permission: at the crossing point both equations describe the same x and the same y — so anything one equation says y equals, you may write in place of y in the other. Below, every single move is shown, one at a time, with the part that changed lit up.
