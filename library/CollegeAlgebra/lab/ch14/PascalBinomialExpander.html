Pascal's Triangle & the Binomial Theorem

You never have to multiply (a + b) by itself over and over. Pascal's triangle hands you the coefficients, and the powers of a and b follow a simple rule. Pick a power, then step through the expansion one term at a time to see exactly where every number comes from.
