A formula like P = 2L + 2W is not written in stone — it is one arrangement of a relationship, and you are allowed to rewrite it so that any letter you like stands alone. The method never changes: look at the variable you want, notice what has been done to it, and undo those things from the outside in. Nothing here is guesswork. Every step is one honest move applied to both sides.
