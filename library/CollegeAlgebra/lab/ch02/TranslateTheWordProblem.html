Translate the Word Problem

A word problem is not a wall of text to be stared at until an answer appears. It is a sentence written in English that can be rewritten, a few words at a time, in algebra. Name the unknown, translate one phrase, then the next, and the equation assembles itself. Pick a problem and step through it — the phrase being translated lights up in the sentence, in the picture, and in the dictionary all at once.
