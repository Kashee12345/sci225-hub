One Operation at a Time

An expression is not a sentence — you do not just read it left to right. Step through each problem one operation at a time, then re-run the very same expression in the wrong order and watch the answer change.

The priority ladder

The two rules. (1) Work down the ladder — finish every operation on a rung before you drop to the next. (2) Within one rung, operations are equal; do them left to right.
