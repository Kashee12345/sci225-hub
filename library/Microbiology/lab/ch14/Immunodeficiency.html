Immunodeficiency

Immunodeficiency is a weakened immune defense — and it comes in two forms. Some people are born with a defect; far more acquire one later in life. The most important acquired cause is HIV, which slowly destroys CD4 T cells. Explore both, then drag the HIV timeline.
