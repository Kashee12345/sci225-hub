What Microbes Eat

Every microbe answers two questions to survive: where do I get energy? and where do I get carbon? Those two answers name its nutritional type — and reveal why some microbes feed on rock and sunlight while every pathogen feeds on you. Choose an energy source and a carbon source to build one.
