Medical vs Surgical Asepsis

Every bedside task uses one of two levels of aseptic technique. Medical asepsis (clean technique) lowers the number of microbes; surgical asepsis (sterile technique) removes them all, spores included. Toggle the two, then tap a task to see which one it calls for.
