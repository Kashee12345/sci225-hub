Antiseptics & Disinfectants

A chemical germicide is defined two ways: by the surface it is safe on — living tissue (antiseptic) versus an inanimate object (disinfectant) — and by its level of activity, meaning how far up the ladder of microbial resistance it can kill. Tap an agent to see both.
