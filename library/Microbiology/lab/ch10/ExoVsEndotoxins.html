Exotoxins vs Endotoxins

Bacteria damage the body with two very different poisons. An exotoxin is a protein a living cell secretes; an endotoxin is part of the gram-negative cell wall, freed only when the cell dies. Pick a toxin, then tap a feature to compare them side by side.
