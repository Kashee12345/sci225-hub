The Four Rights of Stewardship

Antibiotics are a shared resource — every unnecessary or sloppy course helps breed the resistant organisms of tomorrow. Stewardship is the discipline of getting four things right: the right drug, the right dose, the right duration, and the patient adherence that only bedside teaching secures.
