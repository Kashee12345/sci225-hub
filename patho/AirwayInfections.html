What it is

⚠ Don't confuse them: Acute bronchitis is a viral "chest cold" that clears on its own. Chronic bronchitis is a form of COPD — caused by long-term irritants (smoke), it's permanent, not an infection.
Big rule: most upper infections are viral, self-limiting — you just support the patient (fluids, rest, symptom relief). The heavy hitter down in the alveoli is pneumonia, which is usually bacterial and needs antibiotics.
