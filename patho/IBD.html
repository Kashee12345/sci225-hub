Crohn's vs Ulcerative Colitis

The two inflammatory bowel diseases · Pathophysiology Week 13
Both are chronic inflammatory bowel disease (IBD), but they behave differently. Crohn's can strike anywhere from mouth to anus in patchy "skip" lesions that burrow full-thickness. Ulcerative colitis stays in the colon only, continuous from the rectum up, and shallow. Click each.
