What's happening

The dangerous path: an inflamed appendix (or diverticulum, or ulcer) can rupture / perforate → spilling contents into the normally-sterile peritoneal cavity → peritonitis (rigid abdomen, guarding, sepsis). That's why RLQ pain gets taken seriously.
