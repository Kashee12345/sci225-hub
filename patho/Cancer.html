What it means

Grading

How abnormal the cells look under the microscope (differentiation). Well-differentiated = looks normal; poorly = aggressive.

Staging (TNM)

How far it has spread: Tumor size, lymph Nodes, Metastasis. Local → invasion → nodes → distant organs.
