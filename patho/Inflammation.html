Inflammation

The body's response to injury · Pathophysiology Week 3
Inflammation is how the body fights injury and infection — rushing blood, fluid, and cells to the damaged spot. Acute inflammation is short and protective; chronic inflammation drags on and can damage tissue. Click each and meet the key players.

Systemic phase: when inflammation goes whole-body (driven by prostaglandins, cytokines, leukotrienes) you see fever, ↑ WBC count, and changes in vital signs. Short-term trigger = infection; long-term = asthma, lupus, or RA.
