Where's it from?

Upper vs lower: hematemesis, coffee-ground emesis, and melena all point to an UPPER GI bleed (esophagus/stomach/duodenum). Common causes: ruptured esophageal varices, bleeding peptic ulcer, esophagitis/gastritis, gastric or esophageal cancer — often set off by NSAIDs, aspirin, or steroids.
