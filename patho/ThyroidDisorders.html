What's happening

TSH feedback (a favorite exam trick): if thyroid hormone (T4) is LOW, the pituitary senses it and pumps out MORE TSH to push the thyroid — so low T4 → HIGH TSH. Parathyroid caution: the parathyroids sit behind the thyroid, so after thyroid surgery watch for hypocalcemia (carpal spasm / Trousseau's sign).
