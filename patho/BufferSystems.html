How it works

Respiratory rule: breathing controls CO₂ (an acid). Blow off more CO₂ (faster breathing) → pH goes UP. Hold onto CO₂ (slower breathing) → pH goes down. In DKA the body hyperventilates to dump acid — those deep, rapid breaths are Kussmaul respirations.
