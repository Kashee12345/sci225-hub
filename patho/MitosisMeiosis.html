Mitosis vs Meiosis

Two kinds of cell division · Pathophysiology Week 7
The body divides cells two ways: mitosis makes identical copies for growth and repair, while meiosis makes unique sex cells (eggs and sperm) with half the DNA. When meiosis mis-sorts the chromosomes, you get a genetic disorder. Click each.
