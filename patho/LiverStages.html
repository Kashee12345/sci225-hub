Stages of Liver Disease

From inflammation to scarring · Pathophysiology Week 14
The liver takes a hit and inflames (acute hepatitis). If that never settles, it smolders for months (chronic hepatitis), and over years the working liver cells get replaced by scar tissue (cirrhosis) — which is permanent. Click through the stages and watch the tissue change.
