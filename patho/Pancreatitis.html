When the pancreas digests itself · Pathophysiology Week 14
The pancreas makes powerful digestive enzymes, but they\'re supposed to stay switched off until they reach the gut. In pancreatitis they activate too early — inside the pancreas — and it starts digesting itself. Click through normal, acute, and chronic.

What's happening

♂ Men: most often alcoholism
♀ Women: most often biliary disease (gallstones)
Cardinal symptom: severe epigastric pain that radiates to the back, with nausea/vomiting worse after meals (eating tells the pancreas to release more enzymes). Chronic pancreatitis wrecks both jobs: exocrine (enzymes → malabsorption) and endocrine (insulin → diabetes).
