Anemia = not enough healthy red cells (or hemoglobin) to carry oxygen. To sort out the cause, look at the size of the red cells (MCV): small (microcytic), normal (normocytic), or large (macrocytic). Click a type to see its smear and labs.

General anemia symptoms

Whatever the cause, less oxygen delivery gives the same core signs:
😮‍💨 Fatigue & weakness · 🌡️ pallor (pale skin/conjunctiva) · 🫁 shortness of breath on exertion · 💓 tachycardia (the heart speeds up to move what little oxygen there is) · 🥶 cold intolerance, dizziness. Each specific anemia then adds its own tell-tale extras.
