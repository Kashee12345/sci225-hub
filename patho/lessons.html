The Lesson 🫀 Library

Human pathophysiology you can actually play with — the whole course, from fluids & acid-base to immunity, genetics, hormones, and every body system. Each lesson is a hands-on, animated simulation — interpret an ABG, solve a Punnett square, raise the intracranial pressure, stage a burn, build a plaque, read an EKG, trace the bile — with plain-English explanations at every step. Great on a phone, tablet, or projector.

100% free for students & educators

How to use it

Click any lesson below to open it. Nothing to install and no account needed — just tap, drag, and explore. Every tool starts simple (everyday analogies) and layers in the clinical detail, so it works whether you're seeing the topic for the first time or reviewing for an exam.

💡 Tip for instructors: open a lesson full-screen and drive it live in class — change one thing at a time and ask the students to predict what happens next.
