What it looks like

Burn complications: big burns cause massive fluid loss (→ hypovolemic/burn shock), infection (the skin barrier is gone → sepsis), hypothermia, breathing problems from inhalation injury, and later contractures and scarring. The deeper & wider the burn, the higher the risk.
