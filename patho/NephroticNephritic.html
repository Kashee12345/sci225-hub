Nephrotic vs Nephritic Syndrome

What leaks through the glomerular filter? · Pathophysiology Week 11
Each glomerulus is a tiny filter that normally keeps big things — proteins and blood cells — inside the bloodstream, letting only water and waste pass into the urine. It breaks in two ways: nephrOtic leaks prOtein (foamy urine), while nephrItic is Inflamed and leaks blood (cola-coloured urine). Click each to watch the filter.
