Bowel Obstruction

When the gut plumbing backs up · Pathophysiology Week 13
Block the intestine and everything behind the block backs up and swells. Where it happens changes the whole picture: a small-bowel obstruction vomits early and distends a little; a large-bowel obstruction constipates absolutely and blows the belly up over days. Pick a level, then explore what causes it.

The blockage

Bowel loops before the block fill with gas and fluid and swell; nothing gets past to the other side.
