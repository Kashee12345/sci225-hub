What it is

How we test: a Gram stain sorts bacteria by cell wall — gram-positive = thick wall, stains purple; gram-negative = thin wall + outer membrane, stains pink and is harder to treat. A culture & sensitivity then names the bug (culture) and picks the antibiotic (sensitivity).
