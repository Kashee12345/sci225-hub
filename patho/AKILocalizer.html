Phases of recovery

However AKI starts, recovery tends to move through three phases:

1 · Oliguric

<400 mL/day. Waste builds up → ↑ BUN & creatinine, fatigue, malaise.

2 · Diuretic

2–4 L/day. Urine pours out → risk of dehydration & low BP; BUN/Cr stabilise.

3 · Recovery

1–2 L/day. GFR improves; BUN/Cr plateau then fall back toward normal.
