Priority assessment — the 5 P's
PainCan they detect pain? (uncontrolled pain is a red flag)
PulseIs blood circulating past the injury?
PallorPale/cool skin suggests poor perfusion
ParesthesiaCan they feel? (numbness/tingling = nerve)
ParalysisCan they move it?
Trauma comes first — ABCDE primary survey: Airway (occluded?), Breathing (distress? breath sounds?), Circulation (heart rate, pallor, cap refill), Disability (can they respond?), Exposure/Environment (e.g. hypothermia). Highest-risk populations for fracture: the elderly (osteoporosis) and children.
