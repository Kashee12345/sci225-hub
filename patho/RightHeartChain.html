The Road to Right Heart Failure

How a lung problem sinks the right heart — cor pulmonale · Pathophysiology Week 12
Chronic lung disease makes the lungs a high-pressure place to pump into. The right ventricle is only built for low pressure, so over time it strains, thickens, and fails — that's cor pulmonale. Pick a starting cause and walk the chain one step at a time.
