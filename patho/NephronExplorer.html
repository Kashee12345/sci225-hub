Kidney function tests

How we measure whether the nephrons are working:
GFR — Glomerular Filtration Rate
The amount of blood filtered per minute — the best single measure of kidney function. It falls as kidneys fail (end-stage CKD → very low GFR).
Creatinine — a waste product from muscle breakdown that the kidney clears. As GFR drops, creatinine rises → it's used to stage kidney disease.
BUN (Blood Urea Nitrogen) — urea from protein breakdown; it also rises when the kidneys can't clear it.
