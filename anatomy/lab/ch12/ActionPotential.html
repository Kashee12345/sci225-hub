The Action Potential

Reach a threshold of about −55 mV and a neuron fires an all-or-nothing spike: sodium floods in, then potassium floods out. Hit Stimulate and follow the voltage.

Action potential

Resting−70 mV
💡 It's all-or-none: below threshold, nothing happens; at threshold, you get the same full-size spike every time. A stronger stimulus fires them more often, not bigger.
