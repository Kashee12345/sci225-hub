Energy Balance

Body weight comes down to a simple ledger: energy in (food) vs energy out (everything you burn). Tip the balance and see what happens.

Balanced

Energy balancein vs out
Energy out has three parts: BMR (~60–70%, the energy to just stay alive), physical activity (~20–30%), and the thermic effect of food (~10%, digesting it).
