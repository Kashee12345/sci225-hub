Anabolism & Catabolism

Metabolism is every chemical reaction in the body, running in two directions — one builds molecules and stores energy, the other breaks them down and releases it. Switch between them.

Catabolism

Metabolismbuild up · break down
ATP is the cell's energy currency — catabolism charges it (ADP → ATP), anabolism and all cell work spend it (ATP → ADP).
