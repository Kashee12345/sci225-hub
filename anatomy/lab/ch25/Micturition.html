The Micturition Reflex

As the bladder fills, stretch receptors trigger the urge to urinate — but you can override it, at least for a while. Fill the bladder and void it.

Bladder filling

Micturitionfill → urge → void

What's happening

The reflex: stretch receptors → spinal cord → the detrusor muscle contracts and the internal sphincter relaxes. The external sphincter is under voluntary control — which is how you “hold it.”
