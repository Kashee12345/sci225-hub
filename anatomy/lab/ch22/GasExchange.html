Gas Exchange

Gases move by simple diffusion — always from high to low partial pressure. That same rule swaps gases in the lungs one way, and in the tissues the opposite way. Switch between them.

Gas exchangefollow the gradient
Partial pressure (P) is the pressure one gas contributes in a mix. Each gas diffuses down its own gradient — independently of the others.
